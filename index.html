About Vietnam

Vietnam, a country located in the continent of Asia and holds beauty in what it is. Home to tens of thousands of people, 102 million people to be exact. The flag is similar to of China’s being red and having a yellow star in the middle. The official language of Vietnam is Vietnamese ( obviously ). Their form of currency is called Vietnamese “dong”. Compared to USA’s Dollar currency it is 0.000039 United 0.000039 which is a small amount of money if you ask me or anyone! Vietnam is ruled by a communist single party led by general Nguyen Phu Trong.

Foodies

Vietnames has many yummy foods for you which includes Phở (Vietnamese Noodle Soup), considered the national dish. Pho is a delicious noodle soup having a clear broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat . It is often eaten for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Another yummy dish is Bánh Mì (Vietnamese Sandwich). A popular street food, this dish features a crispy baguette filled with various ingredients, typically pâté, Vietnamese sausage, pickled carrots, daikon, cucumber, and cilantro. And if you aren’t full, have some Bánh Da Lợn (Steamed Layer Cake): A chewy, vibrant green-and-yellow cake made from tapioca starch, rice flour, mung beans, and pandan.

Vietnam Beauty Shows

Monuments in Vietnam are more beautiful than anything in the world. Temple of Literature (Van Mieu) (Hanoi) also known as Vietnam’s first university, built in 1070 during the Ly Dynasty. Represents the country's long tradition of education and Confucian, heritage. Another beautiful monument : Golden Bridge (Cau Vang) (Da Nang) A pedestrian bridge opened in 2018 in the Ba Na Hills, designed to look like it is supported by two massive stone hands.A world-renowned modern landmark offering panoramic views of the mountains.

Vietnam Religion

Vietnam mostly uses Mahayana Buddhism as it’s main religion. Believers believe that suffering is caused by desire and attachment, and freedom from this suffering (Nirvana) is achieved through compassion, wisdom, good karma, and the help of Bodhisattvas who are like gods. A famous religion site in Vietnam is Thien Mu Pagoda (Pagoda of the Lady from Heaven) in Hue. It is known For being Located on the banks of the Perfume River, this 17th-century pagoda is iconic for its seven-story Phuoc Duyen Tower. It is one of the most revered spiritual sites in Vietnam, acting as a symbol of Hue and a center for Buddhist worship.
